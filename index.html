Dr. Jacob Thomas

Distinguished Engineer, Visionary Entrepreneur, and Prolific Social Leader.

A Portrait of a Multifaceted Personality

Dr. Jacob Thomas is a shining example of knowledge, experience, professional skill, and humanity. A distinguished Electrical Engineer, visionary entrepreneur, and accomplished social leader, he has made his mark in various fields including engineering, academia, hospitality, and socio-cultural activities for over three decades. Even after retiring from New York City Transit, he remains an internationally respected visiting professor. A dedicated professional committed to the betterment of society and the empowerment of the diaspora community, his contributions to cultural unity and the protection of Indian expatriates' property will always be remembered with admiration.
